#  What is blackjack?

Blackjack, also known as Twenty-One, is a comparing card game between two players. The aim of the game is to score 21 points with the first two cards, without going over 21. The player that does this first wins the hand. Points are scored by counting the value of the cards. Face Cards (Kings, Queens and Jacks) are worth 10 points, aces are worth 1 or 11 points and all other cards their face value.

If both players have 21 points at the end of their hands, it is called a ‘push’ and no money changes hands. However, if one player has more than 21 points, they lose regardless of the other player’s score.
